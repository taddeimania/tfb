Welcome to Tecmo Fantasy Bowl!

If this is your first time here, be sure to check out the about page.


Signing Up

Signing up is easy. Just go to the Login Page and fill out the form. Be sure to give a valid email address, it will be required for activation.


Adding Players

The season is currently in a preseason state - meaning you can poke around the site and add and drop players to your roster as you like but they will be erased when the "Free For All" pickups occur.

When it looks like we've got some full leagues i'll clear your rosters and make you unable to add or drop anymore then I will set a date and time about a week out that the adding of players will be permitted.


Joining a League

There are a few public leagues available to join so once you've created your account you can browse to the "League" button on your tool bar to see a list of available leagues.

Right now communication to your follow league members is kind of limited. In the future I will implement league-wide emails as well as a message board right on your league page.


Matchups

I will generate the matchup schedule for the leagues 48 hours after the free for all pickups are started. Currently a league requires 10 and only 10 teams to be considered a valid league.

I am currently working on allowing leagues of all number teams. Keep an eye on the development blog for more news.
